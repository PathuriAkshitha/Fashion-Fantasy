Designer

Pathuri Asritha

A fashion student with a lifelong fascination for fashion sketching, which has shaped her creative vision. She believes garments are powerful narratives and uses her illustrations to convey storytelling through design and styling. Her work focuses on translating complex ideas into visual fashion concepts, bringing imagined worlds and emotions to life on the page. Each piece is meticulously detailed, with every sketch considered a complete, styled scene. Her approach to design emphasizes the conscious creation of plans and conventions for constructing objects, systems, or human interactions, aiming to solve problems with creativity.
